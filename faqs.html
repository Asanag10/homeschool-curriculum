Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HomeLearn Academy

Getting Started

What is HomeLearn Academy?

HomeLearn Academy is a freemium K-5 homeschool curriculum platform that organizes the best free educational resources into a complete, day-by-day curriculum. We curate content from Khan Academy, PBS Kids, Starfall, and other trusted sources, then add our own lesson plans and teaching guides to make homeschooling simple.

Do I need a teaching degree to use this curriculum?

Absolutely not! Our curriculum is designed specifically for parents without teaching backgrounds. We provide step-by-step lesson plans, teaching tips, and video tutorials (Premium) to guide you through every concept.

How much does it cost?

We offer a completely free tier that includes access to all curriculum overviews, links to free resources, and sample lesson plans. Premium membership starts at $19/month and includes complete daily lesson plans, printable materials, progress tracking, and teaching support. See full pricing details.

Curriculum Questions

What subjects are covered?

All core subjects are included: Reading/Language Arts, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, plus Arts & Enrichment activities. Each subject has age-appropriate content for grades K-5.

How much time does it take each day?

Our curriculum is designed for 3-4 hours of instruction per day for elementary grades. Kindergarten is lighter at 2-3 hours. You can adjust the schedule to fit your family's needs.

Can I use this for multiple children?

Yes! Our Family & Co-op plan (Premium) allows you to use the curriculum with up to 4 children. Each child gets their own progress tracking and customized lesson plans.

Is this curriculum secular or faith-based?

Our curriculum is designed to be faith-friendly and compatible with Christian values. We use academically rigorous, evidence-based resources while ensuring content aligns with biblical principles. All resources are carefully curated to avoid content that conflicts with Christian beliefs.

Technical Questions

Do I need special software or equipment?

No special software is required. You'll need a computer or tablet with internet access to view lessons and access online resources. Some activities require basic supplies like paper, pencils, and household items for experiments.

Can I print the materials?

Premium members have access to printable worksheets, activity pages, and lesson plans. Free members can access and print resources from the external websites we link to (like Khan Academy).

Does it work offline?

Most of our curriculum requires internet access since we link to online resources. However, Premium members can download and print lesson plans and worksheets for offline use.

Premium Membership

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free members get curriculum overviews, resource links, and sample lessons. Premium members get complete daily lesson plans, printable materials, progress tracking, teaching videos, and email support. Compare plans here.

Can I try Premium before committing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Premium with no credit card required. You can explore the full curriculum and decide if it's right for your family.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your Premium membership at any time with no penalties. You'll continue to have access through the end of your billing period.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Premium for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Support & Community

How do I get help if I'm stuck?

Free members can access our community forum to connect with other homeschool parents. Premium members also get email support from experienced educators who can answer specific questions about teaching concepts.

Is there a community I can join?

Yes! All members (free and premium) have access to our community forum where you can ask questions, share ideas, and connect with other homeschool families using HomeLearn Academy.
