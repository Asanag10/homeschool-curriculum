Fourth Grade Achievers!

Perfect for ages 9-10!

Master advanced reading and writing, tackle multi-digit multiplication and division, and explore complex scientific concepts through engaging projects.

What Your Fourth Grader Will Learn

Fourth grade marks a significant transition to more independent learning. Students develop critical thinking skills, work with complex texts, and apply mathematical concepts to real-world problems.

Complete Subject Breakdown

📚

Reading & Literature

  • Analyze themes and main ideas in texts
  • Compare multiple texts on same topic
  • Identify author's purpose and point of view
  • Make inferences using text evidence
  • Read and understand poetry and drama
  • Build advanced vocabulary
✏️

Writing & Grammar

  • Write multi-paragraph essays
  • Use transition words and phrases
  • Write narratives with dialogue
  • Research and cite sources
  • Master complex sentence structures
  • Edit for grammar, spelling, and punctuation
🔢

Math

  • Multi-digit multiplication and division
  • Work with fractions and decimals
  • Understand factors and multiples
  • Measure angles and classify shapes
  • Convert units of measurement
  • Solve multi-step word problems
  • Understand area and perimeter
🔬

Science

  • Study energy and its forms
  • Explore waves, light, and sound
  • Learn about earth's structure and processes
  • Understand ecosystems and food chains
  • Study the water cycle and weather
  • Design and conduct experiments
🌍

Social Studies

  • Study U.S. history and geography
  • Learn about state history and government
  • Explore Native American cultures
  • Understand economic systems
  • Study map skills and regions
  • Learn about historical timelines
🎨

Arts & Enrichment

  • Create complex art projects
  • Learn music theory and composition
  • Develop teamwork through group activities
  • Express ideas through multimedia projects
  • Build physical fitness and coordination

Sample Daily Schedule

A flexible routine designed for 4th graders (4-5 hours of focused learning)

9:00 AM

📖 Reading & Literature

50 minutes - Independent reading, comprehension analysis, literature discussions

9:50 AM

✏️ Writing & Grammar

45 minutes - Writing projects, grammar practice, editing workshops

10:35 AM

🎯 Break & Movement

15 minutes - Snack, stretch, outdoor activity

10:50 AM

🔢 Math

60 minutes - Math lesson, problem solving, practice exercises

11:50 AM

🍎 Lunch Break

45 minutes - Lunch and free time

12:35 PM

🔬 Science or 🌍 Social Studies

45 minutes - Alternating days, experiments, research projects

1:20 PM

🎨 Arts & Enrichment

30 minutes - Art, music, PE, or independent projects
