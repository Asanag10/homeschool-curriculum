Fifth Grade Leaders!

Perfect for ages 10-11!

Master advanced academic skills, develop critical thinking, and prepare for middle school with challenging projects and independent learning opportunities.

What Your Fifth Grader Will Learn

Fifth grade is the capstone of elementary education. Students refine their skills across all subjects, develop strong study habits, and prepare for the transition to middle school with confidence and independence.

Complete Subject Breakdown

📚

Reading & Literature

  • Analyze complex texts and themes
  • Compare and contrast multiple perspectives
  • Identify figurative language and literary devices
  • Synthesize information from multiple sources
  • Read and analyze various genres
  • Make connections between texts and real world
✏️

Writing & Grammar

  • Write well-organized essays with thesis statements
  • Conduct research and cite multiple sources
  • Write persuasive arguments with evidence
  • Use advanced grammar and sentence variety
  • Revise and edit for clarity and style
  • Create multimedia presentations
🔢

Math

  • Master operations with decimals and fractions
  • Understand ratios and proportions
  • Work with percentages
  • Calculate volume of 3D shapes
  • Graph points on coordinate planes
  • Solve algebraic expressions
  • Analyze data and interpret graphs
🔬

Science

  • Study the human body systems
  • Explore chemistry and matter
  • Learn about earth's systems and cycles
  • Understand astronomy and space
  • Study ecosystems and environmental science
  • Design complex experiments and analyze results
🌍

Social Studies

  • Study U.S. history in depth
  • Learn about the Constitution and government
  • Explore world geography and cultures
  • Understand economics and financial literacy
  • Analyze historical events and their impact
  • Develop research and presentation skills
🎨

Arts & Enrichment

  • Create advanced art projects
  • Explore music history and performance
  • Develop leadership through group projects
  • Express ideas through various media
  • Build fitness and team sports skills

Sample Daily Schedule

A flexible routine designed for 5th graders (4-5 hours of focused learning)

9:00 AM

📖 Reading & Literature

50 minutes - Independent reading, literary analysis, discussion and reflection

9:50 AM

✏️ Writing & Grammar

50 minutes - Writing projects, research, grammar, peer editing

10:40 AM

🎯 Break & Movement

15 minutes - Snack, physical activity, brain break

10:55 AM

🔢 Math

60 minutes - Math instruction, problem solving, application exercises

11:55 AM

🍎 Lunch Break

45 minutes - Lunch and free time

12:40 PM

🔬 Science or 🌍 Social Studies

50 minutes - Alternating days, experiments, research, projects

1:30 PM

🎨 Arts & Enrichment

30 minutes - Art, music, PE, or independent study projects
