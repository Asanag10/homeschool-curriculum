🌟 Texas Homeschool Requirements

Legal Requirements

  • Bona fide instruction (not just to avoid attendance laws)
  • Written curriculum in visual form
  • Five core subjects required

Required Subjects & How We Cover Them

Reading

Texas Requirement: Reading instruction

Our Coverage:

  • ✅ Dedicated "Reading & Phonics" section (K-2)
  • ✅ "Reading & Literature" section (3-5)
  • ✅ Daily reading lessons with comprehension activities
  • ✅ Phonics-based instruction
  • ✅ Curated reading lists for each grade
Spelling

Texas Requirement: Spelling instruction

Our Coverage:

  • ✅ Dedicated "Spelling" section at every grade level
  • ✅ Weekly spelling lists with grade-appropriate words
  • ✅ Spelling pattern instruction
  • ✅ Practice worksheets and assessments
  • ✅ Integration with reading and writing lessons
Grammar

Texas Requirement: Grammar instruction

Our Coverage:

  • ✅ "Writing & Grammar" section at every grade level
  • ✅ Parts of speech instruction
  • ✅ Sentence structure and mechanics
  • ✅ Punctuation and capitalization rules
  • ✅ Applied grammar in writing assignments
Mathematics

Texas Requirement: Mathematics instruction

Our Coverage:

  • ✅ Comprehensive "Mathematics" section at every grade
  • ✅ Aligned with grade-level standards
  • ✅ Daily math lessons with practice problems
  • ✅ Hands-on activities and manipulatives
  • ✅ Khan Academy integration for video lessons
Good Citizenship

Texas Requirement: Good Citizenship instruction

Our Coverage:

  • ✅ "Social Studies & Good Citizenship" section at every grade
  • ✅ Character education lessons
  • ✅ Civic responsibility instruction
  • ✅ Community helper studies
  • ✅ Rules, respect, and responsibility themes
  • ✅ Service project ideas

📚 Bonus Subjects (Not Required but Included)

While Texas only requires the five subjects above, our curriculum also includes:

  • Science
  • Arts & Music
  • Physical Education
