Second Grade Scholars!

Perfect for ages 7-8!

Strengthen reading comprehension, master two-digit math, and dive deeper into science and social studies with engaging projects and activities.

What Your Second Grader Will Learn

Second grade expands on foundational skills with more complex reading, multi-step math problems, and deeper exploration of the world. Students gain confidence and independence in their learning journey.

Complete Subject Breakdown

Reading & Literature

45 min/day

Learning Objectives

  • Read chapter books independently with fluency
  • Understand story structure and plot development
  • Make predictions and inferences from text
  • Compare and contrast characters and events
  • Build vocabulary through context clues
  • Read with expression and proper pacing

Resources Used

Khan Academy Reading Library Chapter Books Comprehension Worksheets

Writing & Grammar

40 min/day

Learning Objectives

  • Write multi-paragraph stories with clear structure
  • Use descriptive language and sensory details
  • Understand and use nouns, verbs, and adjectives
  • Apply proper punctuation and capitalization rules
  • Edit and revise own writing for clarity
  • Write informational and opinion pieces

Resources Used

Writing Curriculum ReadWriteThink

Spelling

20 min/day

Learning Objectives

  • Master 300+ grade-level spelling words
  • Learn spelling patterns and word families
  • Understand basic prefixes and suffixes
  • Apply spelling rules in daily writing
Texas Requirement: Spelling is one of the five required subjects for Texas homeschoolers.

Resources Used

Spelling City Weekly Spelling Lists

Mathematics

50 min/day

Learning Objectives

  • Add and subtract within 100 fluently
  • Understand place value to 1,000
  • Skip count by 5s, 10s, and 100s
  • Measure using standard units (inches, feet, cm)
  • Tell time to 5-minute intervals
  • Work with money (coins and bills)
  • Understand basic fractions (halves, thirds, fourths)

Resources Used

Khan Academy - 2nd Grade Math Math Playground Math Worksheets

Science

35 min/day

Units Covered

  • Life cycles of plants and animals
  • Earth materials (rocks, soil, water)
  • Force and motion basics
  • Habitats and ecosystems
  • Weather patterns and climate

Resources Used

PBS Kids Science Nat Geo Kids Experiment Guides

Social Studies & Good Citizenship

30 min/day

Learning Objectives

  • Learn about communities and neighborhoods
  • Study historical figures and events
  • Understand maps, continents, and oceans
  • Explore different cultures and traditions
  • Learn about government and citizenship
  • Study economics (goods, services, needs, wants)
Texas Requirement: Good Citizenship is one of the five required subjects for Texas homeschoolers.

Resources Used

Library Books Social Studies Lessons

Arts & Enrichment

30 min/day

Activities Include

  • Create detailed art projects with various materials
  • Learn rhythm and melody in music
  • Develop coordination through PE activities
  • Express ideas through creative projects
  • Work collaboratively on group activities

Resources Used

Art for Kids Hub GoNoodle Art Project Guides
