Second Grade Scholars!

Perfect for ages 7-8!

Strengthen reading comprehension, master two-digit math, and dive deeper into science and social studies with engaging projects and activities.

What Your Second Grader Will Learn

Second grade expands on foundational skills with more complex reading, multi-step math problems, and deeper exploration of the world. Students gain confidence and independence in their learning journey.

Complete Subject Breakdown

📚

Reading & Literature

  • Read chapter books independently
  • Understand story structure and plot
  • Make predictions and inferences
  • Compare and contrast characters
  • Build vocabulary through context clues
  • Read fluently with expression
✏️

Writing & Grammar

  • Write multi-paragraph stories
  • Use descriptive language and details
  • Understand nouns, verbs, adjectives
  • Use proper punctuation and capitalization
  • Edit and revise own writing
  • Write informational and opinion pieces
🔢

Math

  • Add and subtract within 100
  • Understand place value to 1,000
  • Skip count by 5s, 10s, and 100s
  • Measure using standard units
  • Tell time to 5-minute intervals
  • Work with money (coins and bills)
  • Understand basic fractions
🔬

Science

  • Study life cycles of plants and animals
  • Explore earth materials (rocks, soil, water)
  • Learn about force and motion
  • Understand habitats and ecosystems
  • Conduct experiments and record observations
  • Study weather patterns and climate
🌍

Social Studies

  • Learn about communities and neighborhoods
  • Study historical figures and events
  • Understand maps, continents, and oceans
  • Explore different cultures and traditions
  • Learn about government and citizenship
  • Study economics (goods, services, needs, wants)
🎨

Arts & Enrichment

  • Create detailed art projects
  • Learn rhythm and melody in music
  • Develop coordination through PE activities
  • Express ideas through creative projects
  • Work collaboratively on group activities

Sample Daily Schedule

A flexible routine designed for 2nd graders (3-4 hours of focused learning)

9:00 AM

📖 Reading & Literature

45 minutes - Independent reading, comprehension activities, vocabulary building

9:45 AM

✏️ Writing & Grammar

40 minutes - Writing practice, grammar lessons, creative projects

10:25 AM

🎯 Snack & Movement Break

15 minutes - Healthy snack, stretch, outdoor play

10:40 AM

🔢 Math

50 minutes - Math lesson, problem solving, hands-on activities

11:30 AM

🍎 Lunch Break

45 minutes - Lunch and free play

12:15 PM

🔬 Science or 🌍 Social Studies

35 minutes - Alternating days, experiments, projects, and exploration

12:50 PM

30 minutes - Art, music, PE, or special interest projects
