About HomeLearn Academy

Making homeschool simple, joyful, and effective for families everywhere

Our Story

HomeLearn Academy was born from a simple observation: homeschool parents are drowning in options but starving for organization.

As a homeschool mom myself, I spent countless hours searching for the "perfect" curriculum. I'd bookmark dozens of websites, save hundreds of Pinterest pins, and still feel overwhelmed every Sunday night trying to plan the week ahead.

The irony? Amazing free resources like Khan Academy, PBS Kids, and Starfall already existed. What was missing wasn't content—it was curation, organization, and a clear path forward.

That's why I created HomeLearn Academy. We don't reinvent the wheel. Instead, we take the best free educational resources and organize them into a complete, day-by-day curriculum that actually works for real families.

Our Mission

Simplify

We cut through the overwhelm by curating and organizing the best free resources into a clear, easy-to-follow curriculum.

Support

We provide the teaching guidance, lesson plans, and community support that helps parents feel confident—even without a teaching degree.

Empower

We give families the tools to create a homeschool experience that's educational, enjoyable, and uniquely theirs.

What We Believe

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather give you 10 excellent resources than 100 mediocre ones. Every resource we recommend has been vetted and tested.

Parents Are Capable

You don't need a teaching degree to homeschool well. With the right support and resources, any parent can guide their child's education.

Learning Should Be Joyful

Education works best when it's engaging, hands-on, and tailored to each child's interests and learning style.

Flexibility Matters

Every family is different. Our curriculum provides structure while allowing you to adapt to your family's unique needs and schedule.

Community is Essential

Homeschooling can feel isolating. We're building a supportive community where parents can share, learn, and grow together.

Our Approach

1
