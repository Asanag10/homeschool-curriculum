Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free trial really free?

Yes! You get 14 days of full Premium access without entering a credit card. After the trial, you can choose to subscribe or continue with the free plan.

Can I use this for multiple children?

Absolutely! One subscription covers unlimited children in your household, regardless of which grades they're in.

What if I need to cancel?

You can cancel anytime with no penalties. Monthly subscribers can cancel before the next billing cycle, and yearly subscribers receive a prorated refund within 30 days.

Do I need to be a certified teacher?

Not at all! Our curriculum is designed for parents with no teaching background. We provide step-by-step instructions and video tutorials to guide you.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes! You can upgrade to yearly at any time to start saving. We'll prorate your current month and apply it to your annual subscription.

What resources do I need to buy separately?

Most resources are free (Khan Academy, library books, etc.). For hands-on activities, you may need basic craft supplies and science materials (usually under $50/year).

Is this aligned with state standards?

Yes! Our curriculum is aligned with Common Core and most state standards. Premium members get state-specific reporting guides.

Can I print the materials?

Premium members can print unlimited worksheets, lesson plans, and activities. We also offer digital versions for tablet use.
